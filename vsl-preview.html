Not In Scope

VSL Preview

A video sales letter is not part of the proposed 90-day infrastructure engagement.

Why this is out of scope

The proposed engagement is a focused, time-boxed 90-day AEO + citation-moat infrastructure build. Video production (surgeon-on-camera, procedure-education explainer, before/after reveal) is a parallel discipline with its own production timeline, equipment requirements, and consent workflow.

For a regulated medical practice in Florida, any patient-facing video that names procedures, references outcomes, or shows clinical settings requires legal review against the Florida Board of Medicine, FDA, FTC truth-in-advertising rules, and the ASPS Code of Ethics. The 90-day infrastructure engagement is intentionally scoped to avoid that workflow until the AEO foundation is in place.

What we propose instead in the 90 days

  • Four long-form editorial articles (Phase 2 of the 90-day roadmap), each AI-citation eligible and consent-free since no patient is shown.
  • Press-feature surfacing (SRQ Top Doctors, Sarasota Magazine), already public.
  • GBP Q&A seeding, surgeon-voice but not surgeon-on-camera.

A surgeon-led explainer video, founder-story video, or in-practice tour video is available as a follow-on engagement after the AEO foundation is in place. We will recommend a Sarasota-area medical videographer at that point.
