Not In Scope

Email Sequence

A patient-facing email nurture sequence is not part of the proposed 90-day infrastructure engagement.

Why this is out of scope

The proposed engagement is a focused, time-boxed 90-day AEO + citation-moat infrastructure build. Patient-facing email nurture sequences are a parallel discipline with their own deliverables, timeline, and budget.

The 90-day work is designed to make Google's AI Overview cite West Coast Plastic Surgery as a source on the seven highest-intent searches in Sarasota cosmetic surgery. Patient email nurture is a downstream conversion lever that becomes more valuable once the AI-citation foundation is in place.

What we do address in this engagement

  • One copy variant for the post-consult financing follow-up email, included in the financing trust block deliverable.
  • Email subject-line and body templates for in-practice review requests, included in Phase 3 of the 90-day roadmap.

A full patient nurture sequence (welcome email, education emails, post-consultation cadence, post-procedure cadence, annual recall) is available as a follow-on engagement after the AEO foundation is in place.
